Perl \bib@@title body (BibTeX.pool.ltxml:293-332): re-case a title string while preserving brace-grouped and $math$-delimited regions verbatim. Words are runs of \w chars or \<word>/ \<single-char> control-sequence escapes; whitespace separates words and may modify the next word’s “first” status.

wb (word-beginning) is 1 at the start of a word run; wc (word counter) increments at each word start. Together they let the Capitalize1 mode capitalise the FIRST word and lowercase the rest, while Capitalize uppercases every word.

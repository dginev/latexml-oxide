Versioned dump filename conventions and ambient-TeXLive detection.

All kernel dumps generated by --init=plain.tex and --init=latex.ltx are tagged with the TeXLive release year of the ambient install:

resources/dumps/plain.2025.dump.txt
resources/dumps/latex.2025.dump.txt
resources/dumps/texlive.2025.version

At runtime we detect the ambient year (via kpsewhich -var-value=SELFAUTOPARENT, with pdflatex --version as fallback) and pick the matching dump. If no exact match exists — or no TeXLive is installed at all — we fall back to the most-recent year present.

Why versioned: latex.dump.txt content reflects whatever raw latex.ltx / expl3-code.tex shipped with the ambient TeXLive at --init time. Cross-loading a dump from a different TL into a different TL silently diverges. Tagging by year makes the relationship explicit and lets us bundle multiple TL years into a single binary (see embedded_dumps).

\section{After}
Content after the figure must survive. When subcaption is loaded after
subfigure.sty, its \verb|{subfigure}| environment must not clobber
subfigure.sty's self-contained \verb|\subfigure| macro, or the macro-form call
above reparses as an unclosed environment and swallows the rest of the document.

1//! Stub for colt2024.cls (COLT 2024 — Conference on Learning Theory).
2//!
3//! colt2024.cls does `\LoadClass[pmlr]{jmlr}` and defines `\coltauthor` in its
4//! body: the review form `\newcommand{\coltauthor}[1]{}` (anonymizes), the final
5//! form `\newcommand{\coltauthor}[1]{\author{#1}}`. As an unbound class Rust uses
6//! the OmniBus fallback, which dep-scans the cls (so jmlr loads) but does NOT
7//! execute the cls body — so `\coltauthor` was undefined where Perl raw-executes
8//! the cls and defines it. Route to jmlr (Rust's jmlr binding handles `\editor`/
9//! `\jmlrworkshop`/`\acks`, the rest of the cls body) and define `\coltauthor` as
10//! the final-submission form `\author{#1}` — content-preserving (real author
11//! names, correct for published arXiv versions). Witness 2308.08218.
12use latexml_package::prelude::*;
13
14LoadDefinitions!({
15  LoadClass!("jmlr");
16  DefMacro!("\\coltauthor{}", "\\author{#1}");
17});
